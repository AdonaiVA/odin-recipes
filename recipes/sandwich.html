Sandwich-Odin

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

How to prepare

  1. Heat the bread on a griddle over low heat.
  2. In a medium non-stick frying pan put the slices of ham to brown very lightly on each side.
  3. First spread a slice of bread with the previously mixed mayonnaise and mustard.
  4. Then put the cheese and the ham on top. Cover with the other side of the bread and that's it.
  5. As an extra step you can put the assembled sandwich on the griddle for a couple of minutes on each side so that the heat melts the cheese and everything is wonderful.
